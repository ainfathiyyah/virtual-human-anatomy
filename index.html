Welcome to Virtual Human Anatomy

Virtual Human Anatomy is a Final Year Project designed to enhance anatomy learning through Virtual Reality technology. The application allows users to explore human body systems interactively using detailed 3D anatomical models.

Through immersive learning experiences, students can gain a deeper understanding of the skeletal system, muscular system, and internal organs in an engaging virtual environment.
